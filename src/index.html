Typeface

The AMP logo uses a typeface called Amplify, which was adapted from Audiowide for our team’s use by Mike Sparks, based on modifications by Dave Artz. The primary difference is that Amplify closes the gaps between many of the letterforms. Sparks also created two thinner fonts, a light and an ultralight version.
